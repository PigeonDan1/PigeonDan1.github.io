## Jing Peng (彭景)

I am a Ph.D. student at **Shanghai Jiao Tong University**, **X-LANCE Lab**, advised by **Prof. Kai Yu**.  
My research focuses on **Speech Large Language Models (Speech LLMs)**, especially robust speech understanding under **domain shift** and **multi-speaker** conditions.

## Research Interests

- Speech LLMs for speech understanding and reasoning  
- Speaker-attributed ASR (SA-ASR) and multi-speaker understanding  
- Speech–text alignment for instruction-following speech systems  
- Efficient adaptation for low-resource / cross-domain settings
